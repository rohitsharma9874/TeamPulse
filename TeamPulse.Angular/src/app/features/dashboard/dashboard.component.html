Every work item moves through six stages from intake to completion. Drag cards between columns on the Board to update status instantly.

📥

New

A fresh work item has been logged. No owner or execution scope yet — it is waiting to be picked up and evaluated.

  • Assign a team member
  • Set priority & deadline
  • Move to Refinement when ready to scope

Refinement

The assignee is analysing scope, gathering client information, and clarifying requirements before any execution begins.

  • Collect all client documents
  • Clarify scope & compliance period
  • Move to Ready once scope is confirmed

Ready

Scope is confirmed and all inputs are in hand. The task is queued and cleared for active execution.

  • All documents received from client
  • No open queries blocking work
  • Move to In Progress to begin execution

In Progress

Active execution stage — filings, calculations, or audit work is underway. The clock is ticking against the deadline.

  • Monitor deadline daily
  • Upload working papers & supporting docs
  • Move to Review once draft-complete

Review

Work is complete and awaiting quality check by a senior or manager before submission to the client or authority.

  • Reviewer checks calculations & compliance
  • Client approval obtained if required
  • Move to Complete after sign-off

Complete

Filed, submitted, and closed. The task is archived in the register and billing can be raised if applicable.

  • Acknowledgement / receipt saved
  • Invoice raised against billing value
  • Mark payment status when collected
